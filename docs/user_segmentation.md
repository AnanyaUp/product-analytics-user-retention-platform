# User Segmentation Analysis

## Objective

The objective of this analysis is to identify distinct groups of users based on their engagement and purchasing behavior.

User segmentation helps businesses:

* Understand customer behavior
* Identify high-value customers
* Detect churn-risk users
* Design targeted marketing campaigns
* Improve customer retention and revenue

Two approaches were used:

1. RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
2. K-Means Clustering

---

# RFM Analysis

## Methodology

Three customer behavior metrics were calculated:

### Recency (R)

Number of days since the user's last activity.

Lower recency indicates more recently active users.

### Frequency (F)

Number of user interactions on the platform.

Higher frequency indicates greater engagement.

### Monetary (M)

Total purchase amount generated by the user.

Higher monetary value indicates greater business value.

# Segment Interpretation

## Champions

Highly engaged users with strong purchasing behavior.

These customers represent the most valuable segment.

Recommended Actions:

* Loyalty programs
* Early product access
* Premium offers

---

## Loyal Customers

Users who interact frequently and remain active.

Recommended Actions:

* Personalized recommendations
* Reward programs
* Referral campaigns

---

## Recent Customers

Users who joined recently but have not yet demonstrated strong engagement.

Recommended Actions:

* Onboarding campaigns
* Product education
* First-purchase incentives

---

## Frequent Users

Users who interact frequently but may not purchase regularly.

Recommended Actions:

* Upselling strategies
* Product recommendations
* Conversion optimization

---

## Big Spenders

Users generating high revenue.

Recommended Actions:

* VIP treatment
* Exclusive offers
* Personalized communication

---

## At Risk Users

Users with low recency and low engagement.

Recommended Actions:

* Re-engagement campaigns
* Discount offers
* Personalized notifications

---

# K-Means Clustering

## Methodology

K-Means clustering was applied using:

* Recency
* Frequency
* Monetary

Four clusters were identified.

---

# Cluster Distribution

| Cluster |   Users | Label                |
| ------- | ------: | -------------------- |
| 0       | 270,374 | Casual Users         |
| 1       | 161,221 | Dormant Users        |
| 2       |   1,815 | VIP Customers        |
| 3       |  40,156 | Highly Engaged Users |

---

# Cluster Characteristics

## Casual Users

Average Metrics:

* Recency: 10.23
* Frequency: 1.21
* Monetary: 2.15

Characteristics:

* Recently active
* Low engagement
* Minimal spending

Business Strategy:

Improve conversion and encourage repeat interactions.

---

## Dormant Users

Average Metrics:

* Recency: 23.00
* Frequency: 1.13
* Monetary: 1.83

Characteristics:

* Inactive users
* Low engagement
* Low spending

Business Strategy:

Focus on retention and reactivation campaigns.

---

## VIP Customers

Average Metrics:

* Recency: 13.68
* Frequency: 1.87
* Monetary: 877.25

Characteristics:

* Very high revenue contribution
* Small but valuable customer base

Business Strategy:

Protect and retain this segment through personalized experiences and loyalty initiatives.

---

## Highly Engaged Users

Average Metrics:

* Recency: 10.68
* Frequency: 4.03
* Monetary: 4.73

Characteristics:

* Frequent platform interactions
* Strong engagement behavior

Business Strategy:

Convert engagement into purchases through targeted promotions.

---

# Key Findings

1. A large portion of users exhibit low engagement and low spending behavior.
2. Only a small fraction of users contribute significant revenue.
3. VIP customers represent a high-value segment that should be prioritized.
4. Many users are at risk of churn and require re-engagement efforts.
5. Highly engaged users provide opportunities for conversion optimization.

---

# Business Recommendations

## Retain High-Value Customers

Develop loyalty programs and personalized experiences for VIP customers.

---

## Reactivate Dormant Users

Use targeted campaigns and incentives to encourage return visits.

---

## Improve Conversion

Focus on moving Casual and Highly Engaged Users toward purchase behavior.

---

## Monitor Customer Segments

Track segment movement over time to evaluate retention and marketing effectiveness.

---

# Conclusion

User segmentation revealed distinct behavioral patterns across the customer base.

Combining RFM analysis with machine learning clustering provides actionable insights that can be used to improve retention, engagement, and revenue growth.
